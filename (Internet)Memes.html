Chapter 1: The Meme Lord Welcomes You:

Welcome to the world of Memes. No doubt you've seen them before. Maybe it was a minion on Facebook, or that one friend that always spams your groupchat. We all are familiar with memes, but how much do you actually know about them? This is where our lesson begins. Grab a blanket, your favorite chair, and your personally knitted Shrek doll because we're going to Memeville. Lets get an actual basis of what a meme is. Definitions vary from person to person, but most of them are something like this: A meme is any kind of popular reference from some kind of public media that has been altered or put in a different situation to create something funny. (usually weird as well)

Now that we have a good understandingMemes have adapted and changed over the years. The actual word meme was created by a man named Richard Dawkins in 1972.
Ladies and gentlemen: The first meme by the same man

Memes have grown since then, becoming more and more popular right along with the internet.
The "nest" of memes was a website called "4Chan". 4chan is a great representation of how weird the internet gets, it's filled with insane stories but also memes. The troll face is one example of a 4Chan based meme along with the other "rage faces".
[A rage face is usually a crude Microsoft Paint made image of a grotesque angry face]

Up next we have the classic image macros as seen to the left. When many internet denizens hear "meme", this comes to mind. The first image macro was actually about cats(Once again cats prove their lovability). It was made in 1905 by Harry Whiter Frees. [see right] Their first modern appearance was in a forum called SomethingAweful. It's quite similiar to 4Chan. Users create stories, editted images or just comments. It was a great contributor to the culture of the internet, for example Slenderman originated here. Image macros exploded in popularity between 2005 and started their decline in late 2013. There were many, many, image macro ideas that came and went like Doge, Awesome Baby, and What if I Told You.(see top of screen) .
